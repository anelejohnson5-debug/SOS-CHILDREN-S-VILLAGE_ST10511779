SOS Children's Village

A loving home for every child

SERVICES/IMPACT & BRIEF OVERVIEW OF FAMILIES ASSISTED

SOS Children's Village Ennerdale, established in 1984 near Johannesburg, provides long-term alternative care for about 160 children in 16 family houses, plus a youth facility for 30+ young adults. It offers family-like care, education, and health services, alongside community-based Family Strengthening Programmes to prevent family breakdown and support children in need. 300 children and their families are supported through community outreach work. Children at SOS Ennerdale benefit from consistent and individualised support from the caregivers, social workers and other staff members.
